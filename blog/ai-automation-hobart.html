5 AI Automations Hobart Businesses Need Now

1. Missed Call Text-Back

Whether you're on a job, with a customer at Salamanca, or out in the Huon Valley — every missed call gets an instant professional text response. Saves 60-70% of leads that would otherwise contact a competitor.

2. Quote Follow-Up Sequences

Send a quote and the system chases it at day 1, 3, and 7 — automatically. Hobart customers shop around. Your quote stays top-of-mind. Closes 30-40% more jobs without any manual effort.

3. Google Review Autopilot

Hobart relies heavily on reputation — especially for tourism, hospitality, and trades. Automated review requests after every job or service build your Google reputation consistently, month after month.

4. Appointment Reminders

Confirmation + day-before + 2-hour reminders eliminate 80% of no-shows. Critical for Hobart's large health, wellness, and professional services sector.

5. Invoice Payment Chasers

Automated reminders at 7, 14, and 21 days overdue. Get paid faster without awkward phone calls. Particularly useful for trades doing renovation and construction projects.
