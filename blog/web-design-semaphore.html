AI Automation for Semaphore Businesses

Semaphore businesses get slammed on weekends and holidays. When you're flat out with customers, the last thing you can do is answer every call, chase reviews, or send appointment reminders. AI automation handles it all.

Missed Call Text-Back

Packed house and can't answer the phone? Every missed call gets an instant text. Saves 60-70% of leads and keeps customers happy.

Automated Quote Follow-Ups

For trades and services in the beachside area — automated follow-ups at 1, 3, and 7 days. Closes an extra 30-40% of quotes.

Google Review Autopilot

After every visit or appointment, customers get an SMS asking for a Google review. Build a killer reputation fast — reviews drive foot traffic.

Booking Reminders

Auto confirmation + day-before + 2-hour reminders. Cuts no-shows by up to 80%. Critical for busy weekends.

Invoice Payment Chasers

Automatic nudges at 7, 14, 21 days. Get paid on time without awkward follow-ups.
