The Sunshine Coast population has hit 360,000 and is on track for 500,000 by 2041. It's Australia's fastest-growing metro area outside of the capital cities. 5 AI Automations for Sunshine Coast Businesses

1. Missed Call Text-Back

You're on a job at Peregian Beach or with a client in Noosa. Whoever calls gets an instant text back. 60-70% of those leads stay with you instead of calling someone else.

2. Quote Follow-Up Sequences

Sunshine Coast has a lot of work going around — customers get multiple quotes. Automated follow-ups at day 1, 3, and 7 keep your quote front-of-mind. Closes 30-40% more jobs.

3. Google Review Autopilot

Sunshine Coast residents research everything before they spend. Automated review requests after every job build your reputation month over month, completely hands-free.

4. Appointment Reminders

Confirmation + day-before + 2-hour reminders. No-shows drop 80%. Essential for the coast's large health, beauty, and wellness sectors.

5. Invoice Payment Chasers

Automated reminders at 7, 14, and 21 days. Get paid on time without chasing anyone. Particularly effective for trades doing new builds and renovations.
