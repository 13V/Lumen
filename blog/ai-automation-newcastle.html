5 AI Automations Every Newcastle Business Needs

1. Missed Call Text-Back

Miss a call on-site? The caller instantly gets: "Hey, sorry I missed you — I'm with a customer. What can I help you with? I'll call you back within the hour." Saves 60–70% of leads that would otherwise ring your competitor.

2. Quote Follow-Up Sequences

Send a quote and the system handles chase-up at day 1, 3, and 7. Friendly, professional, completely automatic. Closes 30–40% more quotes without you lifting a finger.

3. Google Review Autopilot

After every job, customers get a personalised SMS asking for a Google review. Hunter Valley people trust reviews — this builds your reputation while you sleep.

4. Appointment Reminders

Booking confirmation + day-before + 2-hour reminder. No-shows drop 80%. Works for trades, health, automotive — any booking-based business.

5. Invoice Payment Chasers

Polite automated nudges at 7, 14, and 21 days overdue. Get paid faster without awkward conversations. Cash flow improves immediately.
