AI Automation for Salisbury Businesses

Salisbury businesses are doing the hard yards — early starts, long days, weekends on the tools. The last thing you need is to spend your evenings chasing invoices and following up quotes. That's where AI automation comes in.

Missed Call Text-Back

On the tools or with a customer? Missed calls get an instant auto-reply. "Hey, sorry I missed you — I'll call back within 30 minutes." Saves 60-70% of leads you'd normally lose.

Automated Quote Follow-Ups

Send a quote, the system chases it at 1, 3, and 7 days. Friendly, professional, and hands-off. Closes an extra 30-40% of jobs.

Google Review Autopilot

After every job, your customer gets an SMS asking for a Google review. Go from 5 reviews to 50+ in a few months. Massive for standing out in Salisbury's competitive trades market.

Appointment Reminders

Auto confirmations and reminders before every booking. Cuts no-shows by up to 80%.

Invoice Payment Chasers

Automatic nudges at 7, 14, and 21 days overdue. Professional, persistent, no awkward calls. Get paid on time.
