5 AI Automations Canberra Businesses Need

1. Missed Call Text-Back

Canberra professionals will not call you twice. When you miss a call, they get an instant professional text response. 60-70% of those leads stay with you instead of Googling again. In Canberra's market, that's high-value work you'd otherwise lose permanently.

2. Quote Follow-Up Sequences

Government contractors and professionals get multiple quotes. Automated follow-ups at day 1, 3, and 7 keep your proposal front-of-mind. Professional, persistent, and completely automated. Closes 30-40% more jobs.

3. Google Review Autopilot

Canberrans research everything. Automated review requests after every job build your Google reputation passively — essential for standing out in a market where customers read reviews carefully before spending.

4. Appointment Reminders

Confirmation + day-before + 2-hour reminders. No-shows drop 80%. Critical for Canberra's large professional services, health, and consulting sector. Saves significant time and revenue lost to empty appointment slots.

5. Invoice Payment Chasers

Automated nudges at 7, 14, and 21 days overdue. No awkward calls. Canberrans pay and expect to be chased professionally — automation handles it seamlessly.
