A missed call doesn’t mean a missed opportunity — unless you let it.

Every single day, Adelaide businesses lose customers they never even knew about. The phone rings while they’re with a client, on a job site, or in a meeting. The caller hears three rings, then voicemail. And just like that, they’re gone — dialling the next number on their list.

But there’s a stupidly simple solution that most businesses don’t know about. It’s called missed call text-back, and it’s the single most effective way to make sure you never lose a lead to a missed call again.

The Real Cost of a Missed Call

Let’s talk numbers, because this is where it gets painful.

The average trade job in Adelaide is worth somewhere between $500 and $2,000. Some jobs — full bathroom renovations, electrical upgrades, commercial painting — are worth much more. Now think about how many calls you miss in a week.

💰
The Maths: Miss just 3 calls a week at an average job value of $500–$2,000. That’s $1,500–$6,000 per week in potential revenue walking out the door. Over a year? That’s $78,000 to $312,000 in business you never even had a chance to win.

And here’s the kicker: most of those callers won’t try again. Research consistently shows that 85% of people who call a business and don’t get an answer will not call back. They don’t leave a voicemail. They don’t send an email. They just move on.

It’s not personal — it’s just how people behave. When someone needs a plumber at 10am on a Tuesday, they want a plumber now. Not in three hours when you check your missed calls.

What Is Missed Call Text-Back?

The concept is beautifully simple. When someone calls your business and you can’t answer, the system automatically sends them a text message within 30 seconds.

The text says something like:

💬
“Hey, sorry we missed your call! We’re currently on a job but wanted to get back to you straight away. How can we help?”

That’s it. No complicated AI conversation. No chatbot weirdness. Just a fast, friendly text that tells the customer: “We saw your call, we care, and we’re here.”

The customer can then reply via text with what they need — a leaking tap, a quote for a paint job, an electrical inspection. And because it’s a text conversation, you can respond when you’re ready. No phone tag. No missed connections. The lead stays alive.

How It Works: Step by Step

There’s no complex setup or tech knowledge required. Here’s exactly what happens behind the scenes:

1️⃣

Call Comes In

A potential customer calls your business number. You’re on a job site, in a meeting, or it’s after hours.

2️⃣

No Answer Detected

The system detects that the call went unanswered. It doesn’t trigger on answered calls — only missed ones.

3️⃣

Auto Text Sent (30 sec)

Within 30 seconds, the caller receives a friendly, personalised text message from your business number.

4️⃣

Customer Replies

The customer texts back with what they need. The conversation continues naturally via SMS.

But it doesn’t stop there. With Lumen’s AI automation, the system can go further:

  • ✓ AI qualifies the lead — asking the right questions to understand what they need, how urgent it is, and their location
  • ✓ You get a summary notification — delivered straight to your phone via Telegram or SMS with the lead’s details, urgency score, and recommended action
  • ✓ You call back the ones worth calling — no more wasting time on tyre-kickers or wrong numbers

Why Text, Not Voicemail?

This is the question everyone asks. “Why not just let them leave a voicemail?”

Here’s why:

💬

98% of Texts Are Read

Within 3 minutes of being received. Texts are virtually impossible to ignore — they sit right there on your lock screen.

📠

Only 20% of Voicemails

Actually get listened to. Most people see “1 new voicemail” and ignore it. They certainly don’t listen to it urgently.

⏱

Texts Are Instant

The customer gets your text in 30 seconds. A voicemail callback might take hours — by which point they’ve already hired someone else.

💭

Texts Are Conversational

People prefer texting. It’s less pressure than a phone call. They can reply in their own time with exactly what they need.

The data is overwhelming. Text messaging is simply a more effective communication channel than voicemail for business enquiries. It’s faster, more likely to be read, and leads to higher conversion rates.

Real Scenario: A Plumber in Adelaide’s North

Let’s walk through a real-world scenario to see how missed call text-back works in practice.

9:15am, Tuesday. Karen in Mawson Lakes discovers water pooling under her kitchen sink. She pulls out her phone and Googles “plumber Mawson Lakes.” She taps the first result — Steve’s Plumbing — and hits call.

9:15am. Steve is under a house in Salisbury, replacing a hot water system. His phone buzzes in his pocket but he can’t answer — he’s holding copper pipe and a blowtorch.

9:16am. Karen’s call goes to voicemail. She hangs up without leaving a message. Without text-back, Karen would call the next plumber on Google. Steve would never know she existed.

9:16am (30 seconds later). Karen’s phone buzzes with a text: “Hey Karen, sorry we missed your call! We’re currently on a job. How can we help?”

9:17am. Karen replies: “Hi, I’ve got water leaking under my kitchen sink. Can someone come today?”

9:17am. The AI responds: “Sorry to hear that! Can you tell me your address so we can check availability? Is the leak constant or just when you run the tap?”

9:19am. Karen provides her address and details. The AI confirms: “Got it — we’ll get Steve to call you as soon as he’s free, likely within the hour. If it’s urgent, turn off the water at the isolation valve under the sink.”

9:20am. Steve gets a Telegram notification with all the details: Karen’s name, address, the problem, urgency level (medium — active leak but contained), and her preferred timeframe.

10:30am. Steve finishes his current job, calls Karen, and books a 2pm visit. Job value: $380. Lead saved in under 5 minutes of automated conversation.

💡
Without missed call text-back: Steve loses a $380 job and Karen calls another plumber. With text-back: The lead is captured, qualified, and booked — all while Steve was busy working. Multiply this by 3–5 missed calls a day and the impact is enormous.

Who Needs Missed Call Text-Back?

If you ever miss phone calls during work, this feature is for you. But some industries benefit more than most:

🔧

Tradies

Plumbers, electricians, painters, builders, landscapers — anyone who works with their hands and can’t answer the phone on a job site.

⚖️

Lawyers & Accountants

Professionals who are in meetings, consultations, or court and can’t take calls during business hours.

🦷

Dentists & Medical

Healthcare providers whose staff are busy with patients. Missed calls mean missed appointments and lost revenue.

🏠

Real Estate Agents

Agents showing properties or in negotiations who can’t take every call. Every missed buyer enquiry is a potential sale lost.

Essentially, if your business relies on inbound phone calls and you can’t always answer, missed call text-back is the most cost-effective solution available. It costs a fraction of a receptionist and captures leads 24/7 — including nights, weekends, and public holidays.
