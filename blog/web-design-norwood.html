AI Automation for Norwood Businesses

Beyond a beautiful website, we set up AI-powered systems that run your business admin on autopilot. Here's what Norwood businesses are automating right now:

Missed Call Text-Back

Busy with a client? On a break? Every missed call triggers an instant SMS: "Hey, sorry I missed you — I'll get back to you within 30 minutes." Recovers 60-70% of leads that would otherwise disappear.

Automated Quote Follow-Ups

Send a quote and the system handles the rest — friendly follow-ups at 1, 3, and 7 days. Professional, consistent, and completely hands-free. Closes an extra 30-40% of quotes.

Google Review Autopilot

After every job or appointment, your customer gets an SMS with a direct Google review link. No awkward conversations. Go from a handful of reviews to 50+ in months — crucial for The Parade businesses competing for foot traffic.

Appointment Reminders & Booking

Automated confirmation, day-before reminder, and 2-hour nudge. Cuts no-shows by up to 80%. Essential for Norwood's medical practices, salons, and professional services.

Invoice Payment Chasers

Automatic reminders at 7, 14, and 21 days overdue. Get paid faster, no awkward conversations. Professional tone, completely automated.
