AI Automation for Prospect Businesses

This is where it gets exciting. Beyond a great website, we set up AI-powered automation that saves Prospect businesses 10-20 hours per week on repetitive tasks. Here's what's possible:

Missed Call Text-Back

You're with a client, on the tools, or in a meeting — and a potential customer calls. Instead of losing that lead, our system instantly texts them back: "Hey, sorry I missed you. I'll call back within 30 minutes." This alone recovers 60-70% of lost leads.

Automated Quote Follow-Ups

You send a quote and then... nothing. The customer ghosts you. With automation, they get a friendly follow-up at 24 hours, 3 days, and 7 days. Professional, persistent, and completely hands-free. Most businesses see a 30-40% bump in close rates.

Google Review Autopilot

After every completed job or appointment, your customer automatically gets an SMS with a direct link to leave a Google review. No awkward asking. Most Prospect businesses go from a handful of reviews to 50+ within a few months.

Appointment Reminders

Client books in? They get an instant confirmation, a reminder the day before, and a nudge 2 hours before. Cuts no-shows by up to 80% — massive for clinics, salons, and any booking-based business in Prospect.

Invoice Payment Chasers

No more chasing overdue invoices manually. The system sends polite reminders at 7, 14, and 21 days — automatically. You get paid faster without the awkward phone call.

The Bottom Line: AI automation isn't about replacing your team — it's about handling the boring, repetitive stuff so you can focus on what you're actually good at. And it runs 24/7, never forgets, and costs a fraction of hiring someone.
