About Lumen ADL

Web design and SEO for Adelaide trades. Built by locals, for locals.

Who We Are

Lumen ADL is a web design and SEO agency based in Adelaide, South Australia. We specialise in helping local trades — plumbers, electricians, painters, concreters, and other service businesses — get found online and attract more customers.

Unlike big agencies that churn out templated websites, we build custom, fast-loading sites designed specifically for trades. We understand that you don't need fancy animations or complicated features. You need a website that loads fast, ranks on Google, and gets the phone ringing.

Why We Started

We saw a gap in the market. Local trades in Adelaide's northern suburbs were being overlooked by digital agencies who either charged too much or delivered cookie-cutter solutions that didn't actually help them get customers.

We started Lumen ADL to change that. Fair pricing, fast delivery, and websites that actually work.

Our Approach

  • No templates. Every site is custom-built for your business.
  • Mobile-first. Most customers search on their phones — your site needs to work perfectly on mobile.
  • SEO included. We don't just build websites; we build websites that rank on Google.
  • Fast delivery. Two weeks from start to finish. No months of back-and-forth.
  • Local focus. We understand Adelaide's northern suburbs market.

Where We Work

We're based in Adelaide and primarily serve businesses in the northern suburbs: Mawson Lakes, Golden Grove, Gawler, Modbury, Para Hills, and surrounding areas. That said, we work with clients across all of South Australia.

What Makes Us Different

Most web agencies in Adelaide charge $5,000+ for templated websites that look identical to your competitor's. We don't. Every site we build is custom-designed for your business, your customers, and your goals.

We're also SEO-first. We don't build pretty sites that no one can find. We build sites that rank on Google from day one. Our approach combines technical SEO, local optimization, and conversion-focused design.

Our Process

Week 1: Discovery & Design. We learn about your business, your customers, and your competition. Then we design a site that speaks to your ideal customer.

Week 2: Build & Launch. We code your site from scratch — no templates, no bloat. Fast, mobile-friendly, and optimized for Google. Then we launch and submit to search engines.

Ongoing: SEO & Support. We don't disappear after launch. Our SEO packages include monthly optimization, content updates, and performance tracking.
